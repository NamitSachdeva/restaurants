Sundarams



Popular for serving authentic south-Indian food, Sundarams Chandigarh is serving people for a long time now. Dream project of a couple from Tamil Nadu who left their job and converted their hobby into a business, Sundarams serves delicious south-Indian dishes. Cozy interiors along with old classical Bollywood numbers playing and a smiling staff are few highlights of this south Indian restaurant in Chandigarh. Must try items out of their menu are masala ghee roast and Mysore masala dosa. Rice papad and rasam is also very delicious.

Address 1: The Eating House, Hotel Aroma, Sector 22, Chandigarh.


Address 2: SCO 35, Madhya Marg, Sector 26, Chandigarh.



Price: Rs. 500 for 2.
